###### Can I withdraw my balance to my bank account?
Yes you can. To do this you must first withdraw your balance into a cryptocurrency wallet such as Kraken. See this step-by-step [guide](/posts/wdc-to-kraken/). From there, you convert it into GBP and you can then withdraw it to your bank account.
